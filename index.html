How It Works

Our technology brings entire communities together into digital health insurance ecosystems centered around Primary Health Centres. With unified enrollment, payment, and benefit management, we ensure equitable access to care at the grassroots level.

Digital Enrollment

Community members can enroll through our agents or via self-service e-forms. Our system ensures easy tracking and automatic grouping around designated PHCs to lower premium costs.

Smart Coverage

Everyone in the community receives equal standardized coverage regardless of pre-existing conditions.

Performance-Based Incentives

PHCs and communities receive performance-based incentives such as solar power kits, equipment, or resident doctors based on meeting enrollment targets.

Flexible Payments

We integrate local economic solutions like produce barter or installment plans to make premiums accessible for informal workers, farmers, and traders.

Interoperability Across States

Our platforms support multi-state integration with SHIAs, enabling continuity of care for mobile populations and real-time data sharing across systems.

Community-Centered Innovation

We leverage feedback loops, local agent networks, and community engagement tools to continuously refine and improve healthcare delivery at the last mile.

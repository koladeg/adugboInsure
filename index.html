How It Works

We bring communities around primary health centres together into a single plan with great benefits at the most affordable rates for all involved.

Enrollment

Community members can enroll through our network of POS agents or directly via our e-forms. With a set number of enrollees per primary health center, premiums are cut by half.

Coverage

All within the community pay the same premium. Those with or without pre-existing conditions are given the same affordable price and benefits.

Rewards

Communities and their Primary Health Centers (PHCs) receive rewards like medical equipment, solar panels and a permanent doctor as enrollment targets are met. The rewards will be tailored to the needs of the community.

Payment Flexibility

Flexible payment options are made available, including quarterly or half-yearly payments. Our partners also assist locals like farmers or traders in selling their products to make payments for premiums.

Multistate Access

Our partnership with state agencies ensure healthcare support across multiple states for enrollees.

Community focused

Along with our partners and agents, both old and new members of the Community Health Insurance (CHI) are brought together to learn from each others experiences to improve health care in the community.
